Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Our approach - satisfaction first.

We focus on getting your website exactly right before it goes live. That’s why we include 2 full revision rounds and a private preview, so you can review everything in detail. Because each website is custom-built, we don’t offer cash refunds once work has started. Instead, we work closely with you to make sure you're fully satisfied before launch.

1. The 50/50 Payment Model

All WebsiteLink Studio packages use a two-stage payment structure designed to protect both parties:

  • First 50% (deposit) — Paid upfront to begin work. This covers the initial design, development, and allocation of resources for your project.
  • Second 50% (balance) — Paid only after you have reviewed the live preview, requested up to 2 revision rounds, and confirmed you are satisfied.

The final website, domain connection, and full access will only be delivered after the remaining 50% balance has been paid in full.

Payment Terms & Refund Policy

Each website is built from scratch based on your specific brief, industry, brand, and goals. Unlike a physical product that can be returned and resold, custom design and development work cannot be reused once created. From the moment we begin, our time, expertise, and resources are dedicated exclusively to your project.

Instead of traditional refunds, we offer a more transparent and client-focused approach: you review a fully functional preview before paying the remaining balance.

3. What You Get Before Paying the Balance

Before any final payment is due, every client receives:

Private live preview — A fully working version of your website on a secure preview URL, accessible only to you.
2 revision rounds — Each revision round must be submitted as a single consolidated list of changes.
Time to review — If no feedback is received within this period, the project will be considered approved.
No pressure — We do not send the final invoice until you confirm in writing (email) that the preview meets your expectations.

Unlimited revisions within 30 days are subject to reasonable use and must remain within the original project scope.

4. Deposit Cancellation Policy

The 50% deposit is non-refundable in the following cases:

  • Work has already begun on your project (design or development started)
  • You decide to cancel after submitting the project brief
  • You fail to provide required content or feedback for more than 14 days, causing project abandonment

However, if we have not yet started work on your project (within 24 hours of payment), you may request a full refund of the deposit. Refunds in this case are processed within 5 business days to the original payment method.

5. Exceptions & Special Circumstances

We understand that exceptional situations arise. In the following cases, we may offer a partial credit or, at our sole discretion, a refund:

  • Force majeure — If we are unable to complete the project due to circumstances beyond our control (illness, emergency, etc.), we will offer a pro-rata refund of the deposit based on work completed.
  • Project scope change — If you decide to downgrade to a smaller package after work has begun, we will credit the difference toward future services.
  • Mutual agreement — In rare cases where both parties agree the project cannot proceed satisfactorily, we may negotiate a fair resolution on a case-by-case basis.

6. Hosting & Maintenance Refunds

Hosting and maintenance fees are billed in advance. If you cancel hosting or maintenance:

  • Monthly maintenance (€50/month) — Cancel anytime. No refund for the current month, but no future charges.
  • Annual hosting (€200/year or €350/year for Online Store) — Cancel within 30 days of renewal for a prorated refund of unused months.

7. Technical Interventions

Hourly technical work (€30/hour) is billed after completion. If you are dissatisfied with the work performed, notify us within 48 hours. We will correct the issue at no extra charge. Refunds for hourly work are not offered once the work has been delivered and accepted.

8. How to Request a Refund or Credit

Send an email to contact@websitelinkstudio.ie with:

  • Your full name and business name
  • Project name or package purchased
  • Date of payment
  • Reason for the request

We respond to all refund requests within 3 business days. Approved refunds are processed within 5 business days to the original payment method.

9. Questions?

We built this policy to be transparent and fair. If anything is unclear, email us at contact@websitelinkstudio.ie and we'll be happy to explain.
